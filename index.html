Our Mission

At Community Action Foundation, we believe that every person deserves the opportunity to thrive. Our mission is to empower individuals and families through comprehensive support programs that address education, health, housing, and economic stability.

Founded in 2010, we have been working directly with communities to identify needs and develop sustainable solutions that create lasting positive change.

Our approach is collaborative, inclusive, and focused on building capacity within the communities we serve.

Our Programs

Education Initiatives

We provide tutoring, mentorship, and scholarship opportunities to help students reach their full potential.

Health & Wellness

Our health programs provide access to preventive care, nutrition education, and mental health support services.

Community Development

We work with local leaders to strengthen neighborhoods through infrastructure improvements and economic development.
